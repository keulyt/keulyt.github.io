Kubernetes Health Probe Demo

A minimal production-style deployment demonstrating correct liveness and readiness probe configuration.

Problem

Many Kubernetes deployments run without properly configured liveness and readiness probes, leading to unhealthy pods serving traffic or delayed failure detection.

Approach

  • Created a minimal deployment manifest with HTTP health endpoints
  • Configured readiness probes to protect service traffic
  • Configured liveness probes to ensure automatic recovery
  • Validated restart behavior through controlled failure testing

Outcome

  • Improved workload resilience
  • Reduced manual intervention during pod failures
  • Clear operational visibility into application health
